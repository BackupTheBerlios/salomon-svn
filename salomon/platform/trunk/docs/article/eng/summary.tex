\section{Summary}
%\section{Podsumowanie}

System \emph{Salomon} is still being developed.
However, as was shown above, even at the current
level its usage was successful.
This sample usage leans toward the conclusion,
that the architecture that was proposed may be
successfully used for this type of problems.

Further works will include:
\begin{itemize}
	\item realization of the scouts mechanism, that permits
	adoption of structure of tasks to the system
	according to the observed project specific (a stage of the solution),
	\item mechanism of task distribution that permits
	taking advantage of distributed architecture
	in a more effective way,
	\item support for other types of knowledge, particularly the rules,
	\item support for other types of data sources (currently only \emph{Firebird} RDBS is supported).
\end{itemize}
