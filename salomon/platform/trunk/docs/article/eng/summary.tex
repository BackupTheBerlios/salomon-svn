the conclusion, that architecture that was proposed may be successfully
used for this type of problems.

Further works will include:
\begin{itemize}
	\item realization of the scouts mechanism, 
	that permits adopting structure of tasks in the system according 
	to the observed project specific (a stage of the solution),
	\item mechanism of task distribution that permits 
	taking advantages of distributed architecture
	in more effective way,
	\item support for other type of knowledge, particularly rules,
	\item support for other types of data sources (currently only \emph{Firebird} RDBS is supported).
\end{itemize}
